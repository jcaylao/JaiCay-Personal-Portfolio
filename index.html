Projects

Grocery & Cooking App

A grocery and cooking app that allows users to easily find new recipes and keep track of their groceries. One feature is recipe tinder, in which users are given a recipe and they can choose to like or dislike a recipe. Another feature is a receipt scanner that allows users to scan their receipts and upload it to their virtual pantry.

Inventory App

I worked with a team to design an inventory management app for restaurant staff to address the problem of inefficient traditional paper logs and spreadsheets. My responsibilities included user research, prototyping in Figma, and integrating feedback into our design.

WSO2 Redesign

I collaborated with a team to redesign the API management system, WSO2, focusing on the challenges developers face when it comes to having to switch between different tools. We created an all-in-one platform where developers can code, test, deploy, and collaborate without having to constantly switch between different softwares and tools.
